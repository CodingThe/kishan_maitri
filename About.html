About Us

We are a team developed this platform where the pricing of the farm products will be predefined by the farmers based on a voting system, by the farmers all over India.

We give the farmers the right  to sell their product directly to the customers instead of an intermediate broker or middle man ,at no charge. Through our platform farmers can also run you businesses (like their dairy products , milk, vegetables and many more) with no involvement of a third-person and they get  a platform of tokenization system in which you can store you product in advance for future to sell when you feel the rate are according to you.
